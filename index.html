About

Putting useful AI in the hands of real people

PrinceLab experiments with automation that stays quiet, thoughtful, and genuinely helpful. I work directly with small community groups to build assistants that respect privacy, reduce repetitive work, and make it easier to share information.

The goal is dependable, human-centred tools that slip into existing workflows instead of demanding new habits or extra management.

Focus Areas
  • Community helpers (like a quiet WhatsApp assistant for local groups).
  • Small tools for organising information and workflows.
  • Automation ideas that remove repetitive manual work.
